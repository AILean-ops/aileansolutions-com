## Product — `/product`

### ↓ Hero

- **Heading:** One email. Every Monday. Four steps before it hits your inbox.
- **Subhead:** Here's exactly what happens between us and your five tracked competitors between Tuesday and Sunday, and what ends up in front of you Monday at 6am local.

### ↓ Steps (section 01)

- **Step 1 · Onboarding week · We find your 5 real competitors.** Not the national franchise five miles over. Not the place that serves a different income band. The five businesses pulling from your actual customer pool. We start with geography, match on service mix and price point, and send you the list before we start tracking. You get final say — swap any of them if our match is off.
- **Step 2 · Tuesday through Sunday · We watch them every day.** Their websites, Google Business Profiles, Instagram, Yelp, Facebook, state licensing boards, and any public pricing pages. Daily snapshots, differenced against last week's snapshot. We don't log into anything we shouldn't. We don't scrape anything that isn't public. This is the boring, patient part. It's the part nobody else does.
- **Step 3 · Sunday evening · We detect what actually changed.** Most "changes" are noise. A new Instagram reel isn't a move. A price card update is. A new injector on the staff page is. A rash of one-star reviews is. We sort signal from noise, rank by operational impact, and write a draft. An operator edits it.
- **Step 4 · Monday 6am local · You read it with your coffee.** Your weekly briefing arrives in plain email. Six-minute read. Three sections: what changed, how you stack up, three moves to consider this week. Written in the voice of someone who's run operations — because it was.

### ↓ Sample (section 02)

- **Heading:** See a full report.
- **Copy:** Radiance Aesthetics is a fictional Phoenix med spa, but the format, voice, and data types are exactly what a live subscriber gets every Monday.

### ↓ FAQ (section 03)

- **Heading:** Things operators ask before signing up.
- **Q: How do you pick my 5 competitors?** Geography first — typically a 3- to 10-mile radius depending on how people actually travel for your service. Then we match on service mix and price band. We send you the list before tracking starts, and you can swap any of them. Onboarding takes about four days.
- **Q: Can I override the competitors you pick?** Yes. Every one of them. We default to matching on service and geography, but you know your market. If you think the salon six blocks away is actually your main competitor because of how referrals flow, tell us and we'll track them instead.
- **Q: How is this different from Google Alerts or Birdeye?** Google Alerts tells you when a competitor's name shows up on the internet — mostly press releases and spam. Birdeye shows you review aggregation, which is one of the nine things we watch. Neither one tells you "they raised injector prices $2 on Thursday and rolled out a Groupon on Friday — here's what we'd do about it." That's the gap we fill.
- **Q: Do I need to install anything?** No. The entire product is an email. No dashboards, no logins, no Chrome extensions, no mobile app. If you can read an email, you have the full product.
- **Q: Is my data shared with other subscribers?** No. Each subscriber's report is generated from public sources about their competitors — we don't ask you for customer lists, pricing, or internal numbers, and we don't share anything across accounts. Your name only appears in our subscriber database.
- **Q: What if my competitors subscribe too?** They'd see reports about their competitors, and you'd see reports about yours. The two reports would contain different information. If two subscribers happen to track each other, the system handles that without exposing either one's subscription.
- **Q: How much operator review goes into each report?** Every report. Brian personally reviews the draft of every Monday email before it sends during the first 12 months of each vertical launch. Automation handles the watching and drafting. An operator signs off on the recommendations.
- **Q: What if I cancel?** You cancel. We stop sending. We retain your email address for 30 days in case you want to reactivate, then purge everything except what we need for tax records. There is no contract, no cancellation fee, and no "retention specialist" who will call you.
- **Q: Will there be a phone call or demo I need to sit through?** Not unless you want one. The entire sales process is: you read the site, you read a sample report, you decide, you sign up. Brian will reply to any email you send him, but we don't schedule demos, discovery calls, or "quick intro chats."
- **Q: What industries does this work for?** Any local service business where a handful of local competitors matter more than national chains. Med spas, dental specialty, veterinary, boutique fitness, HVAC, home services, specialty retail, private practice healthcare. We're launching one vertical at a time so the reports are sharp. Tell us where to go next.
- **Q: How much does it cost?** Pricing is sent in response to your waitlist signup or a direct email to Brian. We're intentionally not listing it on the site — founding-member pricing is different from standard pricing, and we want to talk through which applies before sending numbers.
